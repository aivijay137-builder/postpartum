Attention Needed Breast

My breasts are rock hard and painful

clinical_notes Clinical: Engorgement

What this likely is

Breast engorgement is the painful overfullness of the breasts. It occurs when milk, blood flow, and lymphatic fluids increase in the breast tissue.

This is extremely common in the first few days postpartum as your body transitions from colostrum to mature milk production.

Immediate relief steps

1

Warm compress before feed

Apply for 5 mins to help dilate ducts and encourage milk flow.

2

Gentle massage

Use light, circular strokes towards the nipple area while nursing or pumping.

3

Frequent feeding

Don't wait for hunger cues; aim to drain the breast every 2-3 hours.

check_circle Do's
  • • Hand express for comfort
  • • Use cold packs after feeds
  • • Wear a supportive bra
cancel Don'ts
  • • Limit fluid intake
  • • Skip night feeds
  • • Use tight underwire bras
warning

Red flags - see a doctor if...

  • High fever over 101.3F
  • Red streaks appearing on breast tissue
  • Flu-like symptoms (chills, sudden aches)
hourglass_empty

When to expect improvement

With consistent management and frequent milk removal, engorgement usually resolves within 24-48 hours. Your milk supply will soon regulate to meet your baby's needs.
